#### Apache

RewriteEngine On
RewriteLogLevel 9
RewriteLog "logs/rewrite.log"
RewriteRule ^(/assets|/css|/images|/themes|/js|/favicon\.ico|/crossdomain\.xml|/upload).* $0 [L]
RewriteCond %{QUERY_STRING} ^(.*)$
RewriteRule ^([^\?]*)$  /index-dev.php$1?%1 [L]
